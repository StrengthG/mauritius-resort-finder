## Personalisation Tips

- **Always read at least one recent post** on the site before emailing. Drop
  one genuine sentence referencing it — e.g. *"I enjoyed your recent piece on
  Bali — the Mauritius comparison section was spot on."*

- **Match the DA to the template type.** DA 80+ sites won't reply to guest post
  pitches unless you have a strong portfolio. Use resource-link or broken-link
  for the big players.

- **Never send bulk BCC.** One personalised email outperforms ten generic ones.

- **Keep subject lines under 50 characters.** "Broken link on your Mauritius
  page" outperforms "I noticed an issue on your website that I'd like to bring
  to your attention."

- **Plain text converts better than HTML.** No fancy signatures, no logos.

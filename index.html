Scoring Framework

How we score hotels

Every score is reproducible. Here is exactly what goes into it.

The four pillars

Each hotel is scored across four criteria, each weighted independently. No pillar is hidden. Final composite score is the mean of all four, rounded to one decimal place.

📍

Location score

Beach quality, reef access, proximity to attractions, transfer distance from Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam International Airport, and surrounding environment.

🏊

Amenity score

Spa facilities, pool size and quality, dining options and cuisine standard, watersports availability, kids club, butler service, and fitness facilities.

🏷️

Brand credibility

Chain reputation, consistency across properties, staff training investment, and independent accreditation (Forbes Travel Guide, Leading Hotels of the World, etc.).

💰

Value score

What you actually get relative to the nightly rate. A 9.2 hotel charging four times the rate of an 8.4 hotel will score lower on value, and we show you that trade-off explicitly.

What we don’t do

✕
Accept sponsored placements No hotel has paid to appear here. No hotel has paid to rank higher. Rankings are purely score-driven.
✕
Use hotel-supplied press quotes We don’t repeat what a hotel’s PR team writes. Guest review data and independently verified criteria drive every score.
✕
Inflate scores to protect affiliate revenue Some hotels we link to score lower than ones we don’t. The score is the score.
✕
Hide our affiliate relationships “Check availability →” links are Expedia affiliate links. We earn a commission if you book. It does not affect rankings.
✕
Show stale prices We don’t display nightly rates on this site. Mauritius prices shift daily. “Check availability” links to live Expedia rates for your exact dates.
About this site

Built to cut through the noise

Mauritius hotel search is broken. Every “Best Hotels in Mauritius” article you find was written by someone who’s never been, or paid for by the hotels themselves. Booking platforms sort by commission rate. Review sites are gamed by incentivised guests.

We built Mauritius Resort Finder to answer one honest question: if you’re spending serious money on a Mauritius holiday, which resort will actually deliver?

Our scoring pulls from 21,000+ verified guest reviews across 36 properties, combined with independently assessed criteria for location, amenities, brand quality, and value. Every score is visible, every pillar is broken out, and every methodology decision is documented above.
